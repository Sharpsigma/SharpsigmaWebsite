How We Work

Structured engagements, defined outcomes, complete accountability. Every SharpSigma engagement follows a clear process — so you always know where things stand.

Engagement Models

Three Ways to Engage

01 📦
Turnkey Delivery

Give us a drawing or a requirement. We deliver finished, inspected parts — managing all production complexity on your behalf. Full accountability. Zero operational burden on your end.

02 🏭
Consultancy Project

A defined scope with measurable deliverables. We come in, do the work — quality systems, facility design, cost engineering, lean implementation — document it, and hand it over properly.

03 🔗
CM Partnership Program

The most comprehensive engagement — full lifecycle contract manufacturing management. We build, qualify, and manage your CM supply chain from search through serial production.

The CM Lifecycle

Full Contract Manufacturing Lifecycle

Most firms offer pieces of this. SharpSigma manages the entire CM lifecycle — from the first requirements conversation through long-term serial production management. Eight structured phases, one accountable partner.

01
Phase One
Requirements Definition

We begin by mapping your manufacturing requirements in full detail — product categories, volumes, quality standards, lead time expectations, geographic constraints, and long-term strategic objectives. A clear requirements baseline prevents misalignment at every subsequent phase.

02
Phase Two
Market Search & Identification

We identify manufacturing partners that fit your requirements — drawing on industry knowledge, technical networks, and structured search criteria. Candidates are screened for capability, capacity, financial stability, quality track record, and cultural fit before any technical evaluation begins.

03
Phase Three
Technical Audit & Assessment

On-site technical audit of shortlisted candidates. We assess manufacturing capability and equipment, quality management systems, process controls, capacity and scalability, workforce competency, and supply chain depth. Our engineering and quality expertise means we evaluate at a technical depth most procurement teams can't match.

04
Phase Four
Qualification

Formal supplier qualification against your specific requirements and applicable industry standards. Sample part production, first-article inspection, capability studies, and documented qualification reports. We don't recommend a partner to you until they've earned it through the process.

05
Phase Five
Contract & Relationship Structuring

We help structure the commercial and quality framework of the CM relationship — pricing models, quality clauses, delivery and lead-time terms, non-conformance and escalation processes, IP protections, and exit provisions. A well-structured agreement prevents the majority of CM relationship failures.

06
Phase Six
Launch & Ramp-Up Oversight

We oversee the initial production runs — first-article approval, process validation, initial quality monitoring, and ramp-up to target volume and quality levels. We stay through this phase until performance is demonstrably stable. Handoff happens when it's genuinely earned, not on a calendar date.

07
Phase Seven
Continuous Improvement

Serial production is never static. We support ongoing performance improvement — corrective action management, process improvement initiatives, cost reduction programs, and quality system development at your CM partner. The relationship improves over time, not just maintains.

08
Phase Eight
Serial Management & Re-Audit

Long-term supplier relationship management — periodic re-audits, capacity planning, performance scorecarding, and proactive risk management. We maintain the oversight that keeps your supply chain resilient as volumes, products, and market conditions evolve.
